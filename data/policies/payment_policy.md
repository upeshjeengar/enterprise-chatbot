# Payment Policy (v2.1)

**Owner:** Finance & Treasury
**Applies to:** Release of payments to vendors.

## Section 1. Dual Control
- **Section 1.1** — Every payment requires two distinct human approvers (maker-checker). An AI agent is never one of the approvers.
- **Section 1.2** — Payment can only be released after the associated purchase request is fully approved and the vendor is fully onboarded.

## Section 2. Prohibited Automatic Actions
- **Section 2.1** — The agent must **never** approve or release a payment automatically. This is a hard block.
- **Section 2.2** — The agent may prepare a payment packet (invoice reference, PO reference, amount) for human review.

## Section 3. Thresholds
- Payments above ₹10,00,000 require CFO co-approval in addition to Finance Manager.
