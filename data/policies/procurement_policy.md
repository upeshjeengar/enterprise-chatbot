# Procurement Policy (v3.2)

**Owner:** Finance Department
**Applies to:** All purchase requests and vendor contracts at AcmeCorp.

## Section 1. Purpose
This policy governs how purchase requests and vendor contracts are authorized. It defines spending approval thresholds and required reviews.

## Section 2. Approval Thresholds
All amounts are in Indian Rupees (INR).

- **Section 2.1** — Purchases **below ₹2,00,000 (2 lakh)** require **Manager** approval only.
- **Section 2.2** — Purchases from **₹2,00,000 to ₹5,00,000 (2–5 lakh)** require **Department Head** approval.
- **Section 2.3** — Purchases **above ₹5,00,000 (5 lakh)** require **Finance Manager** approval.
- **Section 2.4** — Purchases **above ₹10,00,000 (10 lakh)** additionally require **CFO** approval.

## Section 3. Mandatory Reviews
- **Section 3.1** — Any contract with a new (first-time) vendor requires the vendor onboarding process to complete first (see Vendor Onboarding Policy).
- **Section 3.2** — Any purchase that grants the vendor access to internal systems requires a Security review before access is provisioned.

## Section 4. Prohibited Automatic Actions
- **Section 4.1** — An AI agent may **draft** a purchase requisition and **create** a procurement ticket, but must **never auto-approve** a purchase or release payment.
- **Section 4.2** — Payment release is always a human, dual-control action.

## Section 5. Audit
Every procurement action must be logged with requester, amount, approvers, and the policy sections applied.
