# Vendor Risk Policy (v1.3)

**Owner:** Procurement & Information Security
**Applies to:** Risk scoring of third-party vendors.

## Section 1. Risk Factors
A vendor's risk level is derived from:
- **Contract value** — above ₹5,00,000 raises risk.
- **Data access** — access to Confidential or Restricted data raises risk.
- **System access** — access to internal systems or source code raises risk.
- **Geography / data residency** — offshore data processing raises risk.

## Section 2. Risk Levels
- **Low** — no data/system access, under ₹2,00,000. Fast-track allowed.
- **Medium** — some internal access OR value ₹2–5,00,000. Standard review.
- **High** — Confidential/Restricted data access OR value above ₹5,00,000. Full Legal + Security + Finance review.

## Section 3. Controls by Risk
| Risk | Required controls |
|---|---|
| Low | Vendor profile, Department Head approval |
| Medium | + NDA, Security review |
| High | + Legal review, Finance/CFO approval, DPO review if PII |

## Section 4. Agent Guidance
The Compliance & Risk agent computes the risk level deterministically from these factors and never lowers it based on vendor-supplied text.
