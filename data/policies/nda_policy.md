# NDA & Legal Review Policy (v1.8)

**Owner:** Legal Department
**Applies to:** Non-disclosure agreements and legal review of vendor contracts.

## Section 1. When an NDA is Required
- **Section 1.1** — Any vendor receiving confidential, proprietary, or customer information requires a mutual NDA.
- **Section 1.2** — Standard NDA template (LEG-TMPL-NDA-STD) is used unless the vendor proposes redlines.

## Section 2. Legal Review Triggers
- **Section 2.1** — Contracts above ₹5,00,000 require Legal review of terms.
- **Section 2.2** — Any non-standard NDA, custom SLA, or data processing addendum requires Legal review.

## Section 3. Agent Boundaries
- **Section 3.1** — The agent may **draft** an NDA request and **create** a legal review ticket.
- **Section 3.2** — The agent must **never** mark an NDA as executed/signed, and must **never** send a contract to an external email address without Legal approval.

## Section 4. Sequence
Draft NDA request → route to Legal → Legal review → executed NDA → vendor may proceed to access provisioning.
